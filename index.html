    Peixin Lu (路培鑫)

    lupx@whu.edu.cn [Google Scholar]

    Hi, I am a Ph.D. student in School of Information Management under the supervision of Prof. Long Lu at Wuhan University. My main focus of research is the integration of multi-modal data for disease diagnosis and prediction. To assist Alzhemier's disease prediction, I am integrating imaging (MRI), genetic (SNP) and clinical (EHR) data. We first proposed a transferring learning and contrastive learning based neural network model to resolve the small-sample-size issue, which was recently published on Frontiers in Aging Neuroscience. We’re currently developing an attention model for multi-modal integration for better prediction performance.
